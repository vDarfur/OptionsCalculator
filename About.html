About

Options pricer takes user provided information about an options contract such as the greeks, holding period, and anticipated price. Other programs that calculate option prices don't consider implied volatility making earnings plays more vague and risky. With options pricer, Vega and Implied volatility are both considered so traders aren't blindsided by IV Crush. IV crush is an important factor to consider when trading options since even large swings in the right direction can still leave traders at a loss. By anticipating changes in implied volatility, Options Pricer makes earnings plays slightly less daunting.

How does it calculate?

Options pricer takes user provided information about an options contract such as the greeks, holding period, and anticipated price. Other programs that calculate option prices don't consider implied volatility making earnings plays more vague and risky. With options pricer, Vega and Implied volatility are both considered so traders aren't blindsided by IV Crush. IV crush is an important factor to consider when trading options since even large swings in the right direction can still leave traders at a loss. By anticipating changes in implied volatility, Options Pricer makes earnings plays slightly less daunting. To calculate anticipated losses to IV crush before an earnings play, anticipate a 44% drop in implied volatilty after an earnings release.

Limitations

As of now, delta is recalculated using gamma only after entire dollar increments. For example, if I wanted to calculate the price of a security as the value increased from $1 to $1.50, delta would not be recalculated since the price change was less than a dollar. Additionally, as of now theta is treated as a constant (obviously theta is not a constant in practice) so as holding period increases, the calculator becomes less accurate. Finally, gamma is treated as a constant as well (working on fixing this) so as the price in the underlying security changes, gamma doesn't change at all. I will continue to work on these limitations.
